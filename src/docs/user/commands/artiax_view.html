Command: artiax view

Usage:
artiax view [ direction ]

The artiax view command sets the current camera position to a standard viewing direction. For example: artiax view xz brings the camera position in the xz axis view to the current tomogram.

The keyword direction specifies which of the standard viewing directions will be used. When not specified, direction defaults to 'xy'.

direction meaning
xy orients the camera such that the x-axis points to the right, the y-axis points up, and the z-axis points through the screen.
xz orients the camera such that the x-axis points to the right, the z-axis points up, and the y-axis points away from the screen.
yz orients the camera such that the y-axis points to the right, the z-axis points up, and the x-axis points through the screen.

Examples:

artiax view
artiax view yz
